goblin_cbm_runner.cbm.data_processing.geo_processing.geo_FM_disturbances

This module manages disturbances within the Carbon Budget Modeling (CBM) framework, specifically tailored for scenarios involving afforestation areas at the catchment level, both legacy and scenario-specific disturbances. It organizes and processes disturbance data to support the simulation of forest dynamics under varying management and disturbance scenarios.
